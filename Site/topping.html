Drink toppings

Tapioca Pearls

Tapioca, also known as “bubbles” or “boba,” is a fan favorite. It is made from tapioca starch boiled in water. Tapioca has a chewy and squishy texture, and is for the most part, tasteless

Popping Bubbles

Unlike our tapioca based chewy bubbles, our popping bubbles burst juice in your mouth for a sweet splash of flavor. Popping bubble has a thin, gel-like skin with fresh juice inside that bursts when squeezed.

  • Mango popping bubbles
  • Strawberry popping bubbles
  • Bluberry popping bubbles
  • Pineapple popping bubbles
  • Peach popping bubbles
  • Kiwi popping bubbles
  • Coffee popping bubbles

Pudding

Our pudding topping is made with whole milk and sugar and has a smooth and jelly like texture. The flavor is sweet and milky

  • Vanila pudding
  • Choccolate pudding

Red Bean

Red bean, also known as adzuki bean, is a topping made from dried red beans and sugar. The flavor can be described as sweet, creamy and earthy. It has a slight granulated texture and is high in fiber.

Aloe Vera

An assorted aloe flavored jelly cubes that can be added to any drink to boost the flavor of your tea. Aloe Jelly contains rich natural nutrients and fiber which is beneficial for you internal body.

Lychee

Lychee is a small fruit commonly found in Asia. This jelly is made from the inside layer of a coconut, with a texture is slightly thicker than gelatin, and sweet flavor.
