BUBBLE TEA

Milk Tea (Iced or Hot)

Milk Teas use non-dairy milk or milk powder with water and/or green or black tea

  • Taro Milk Tea
  • Jasmine Green Milk Tea
  • Honeydew Milk Tea
  • Classic Black Milk Tea
  • Almond Milk Tea
  • Red Bean Milk Tea

Fresh Milk Tea (Iced or Hot)

Fresh Milk Teas use either regular milk or whole milk. The consistency of fresh milk tea is usually thicker than milk tea.

  • Black Tea & Fresh Milk
  • Green Tea & Fresh Milk
  • Wintermelon & Fresh Milk
  • Vanila Chai & Fresh Milk

Fruit Tea

Fruit Teas are a mix of fruit juices and/or fruit flavored green or black tea.

  • Mango & Passionfruit Tea
  • Fruit Garden Tea
  • Blueberry Green Tea
  • Peach Green Tea
  • Mango Green Tea
  • Grapefruit Green Tea
  • Strawberry Black Tea
  • Passionfruit Green Tea
  • Green Apple Tea
  • Kumquat & Lemon Tea

Classic Tea (Iced or Hot)

Classic Teas are a traditionally flavored, lighter tasting drink, with a green tea or ginger base

  • Classic Green Tea
  • Honey Green Tea
  • Ginger Tea

Snow

A Snow is a sweet mix of blended ice, fruit, and non-dairy milk or milk powder

  • Strawberry & Banana Snow
  • Mango & Banana Snow
  • Peach & Banana Snow
  • Pineapple & Banana Snow
  • Blueberry & Banana Snow
  • Mango, Pineapple & Banana Snow
  • Strawberry, Peach & Banana Snow
  • Veryberry Snow

Yakult

Yakult is a mixture of a popular Japanese probiotic yogurt beverage with fruit juice.

  • Mango Yakult
  • Peach Yakult
  • Passionfruit Yakult
  • Kumquat & Lemon Yakult
