What is Bubble Tea?

Ming's Bubble Tea is a veteran owned small business in Kent. At Ming's Bubble Tea, we only use premium loose tea leaves, no tea powders. We brew all of our teas in house. We make all of our drinks with fresh tea. We strive for authenticity. Our goal is to bring the highest quality tea and the finest taste to northeast Ohio.
